How to Properly Invoice Your Customer: A Guide
For many businesses, invoicing is viewed as a rather perfunctory action. An invoice is often given little thought or importance, and that’s a recipe for trouble. As a factoring company who processes thousands of invoices a month, we know how critical a properly constructed invoice is to the health of your business. If you want to get the most out of your business, and your customers, it’s time to implement best practices and ensure that your company follows them. To understand why this is such a critical step – and to see how so many businesses get it wrong – let’s dig a little deeper into the role of the invoice in your business.
